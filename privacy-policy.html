Pomelo strongly believes in the protection of personal information, and we understand that people have different levels of comfort with sharing personal information. Therefore, we limit the information we collect at the level of specificity that is believed to be useful or to improve the usability and reliability of our services. All personal information entered into a Pomelo Application or Website by a Service Provider will not be independently verified by Pomelo.

In particular:

  • We encrypt personalized areas of our Platforms using SSL.
  • We regularly review and upgrade our information collection, storage and processing practices, including physical security measures, to guard against unauthorized access to systems.
  • We restrict access to personal information to Pomelo employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.

Please note that emails, instant messaging, and similar means of communication with Pomelo are not encrypted, and we strongly advise you not to communicate any confidential information through these means.
